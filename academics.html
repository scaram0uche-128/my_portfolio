About College

Indian Institute of Science Education and Research (IISER), Kolkata

Founded in 2006 by the Ministry of Education (Government of India), IISER Kolkata is a premier institute dedicated to integrating world-class education with cutting-edge research in the basic sciences. Declared an Institute of National Importance in 2012, IISER Kolkata stands among the leading scientific institutes of the country.

Programs span disciplines such as Biological Sciences, Chemical Sciences, Physical Sciences, Earth Sciences, Mathematics & Statistics, Computational & Data Sciences, and Humanities & Social Sciences.
IISER Kolkata combines academic excellence, cutting-edge research, and vibrant student life to nurture the next generation of scientists and innovators.
